Dr. Yewen Chen

Yewen Chen, PhD in Statistics, joined Dr. Shen's lab as a Postdoctoral Associate in December 2023. His interests are in the development of hierarchical dynamic prediction modeling techniques along with high-speed algorithms. These methods can typically be applied to post-processing outputs of numerical models, such as fine particulate matter concentrations and rainfall amounts. Additionally, these methods can be used in hotspot predictions of high-transmission areas for Schistosomiasis. He is also interested in Bayesian variable selection and semiparametric modeling methods in spatial statistics.
